GET TO KNOW US

Welcome to the vibrant and innovative Computer Science department at University of Ghana. Our undergraduate program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive and cutting-edge education in computer science, preparing them for exciting careers in the ever-evolving world of technology.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

We strive to admit students who are passionate about computing and have a strong academic background. Please review our admission requirements and procedures to ensure a smooth application process. We look forward to welcoming motivated individuals to join our dynamic community.

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Our Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program is crafted to equip students with a strong foundation in computer science principles, programming languages, and problem-solving skills. With a diverse range of courses and elective options, students have the flexibility to tailor their education to their interests.
